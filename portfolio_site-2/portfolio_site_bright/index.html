About Me

As a KS1 teacher and early childhood educator, I strive to spark confidence, curiosity and a love of learning in every child. With over six years of experience in Moscow preschools and language schools, I have developed a philosophy that blends play‑based and inquiry‑based activities with phonics, storytelling and hands‑on science and maths explorations. By weaving cross‑curricular connections into my lessons, I help children see how language, numeracy and science fit together in the world around them.

I collaborate closely with parents to support each child’s development and provide regular progress updates. Multicultural communication skills and my B1 level Russian help me connect effectively with families from diverse backgrounds and ensure that our home–school partnership nurtures both social and academic growth.
