About Me

As an early childhood English teacher and caregiver, I strive to foster confidence and curiosity in every child. With more than six years of experience in Moscow preschools and language schools, I have honed a play‑based teaching philosophy that emphasizes phonics, storytelling and hands‑on activities. My work blends nurturing care with structured routines so children feel safe to express themselves, make friends and build foundational language skills.

I collaborate closely with parents to support each child’s development and provide regular progress updates. Multicultural communication skills and my B1 level Russian help me connect effectively with families from diverse backgrounds.
